Canonical structures

A canonical structure declares "canonical" conversion hints between the effective components of a structure and the projections of the structure

type cs_pattern =
| Const_cs of Names.GlobRef.t
| Prod_cs
| Sort_cs of Sorts.family
| Default_cs

A cs_pattern characterizes the form of a component of canonical structure
